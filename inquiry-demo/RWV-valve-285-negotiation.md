# 预判谈判 — RED-WHITE VALVE CORP.

## 客户背景
- Jeremy Beck, Senior Purchasing Manager, 7年+经验
- 公司年采购$20M+，母公司意大利VIR
- 主要供应商是意大利母公司，中国供应商是补充

## 预判场景及应对

### 场景1: "Your trial price is too high — we pay less from our Italian supplier"

**预判概率**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 很高

**应对策略**: 不要直接降价。Jeremy管$20M采购，他懂成本结构。

```
Hi Jeremy,

I understand your Italian supplier may have different pricing, 
and a direct comparison can be tricky. Here are a few things 
to consider:

1. Are you comparing FOB to FOB? Our $89 includes CMM reports 
   and full inspection — some suppliers charge extra for this.

2. The trial price reflects one-time setup costs ($400 fixturing 
   + $150 programming). These won't apply to production orders, 
   which is why the 200pc price drops to $67.

3. For the ongoing 200-500 pcs/month, we're happy to do a 
   cost breakdown comparison if you share what's included in 
   your current supplier's price.

Our goal isn't to be the cheapest — it's to be the most 
reliable backup when your primary source can't keep up.

Would a cost comparison call be useful?
```

### 场景2: "Can you do $75/pc for the trial order?"

**预判概率**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 高

**应对策略**: 可以小幅让步，但换取条件

```
Hi Jeremy,

We can meet at $82/pc for the trial order with one adjustment:

If you add the two locating holes I suggested in the DFM notes 
on your next casting run, it saves us setup time on every batch. 
This brings the production price down further:

| Qty | Revised Price |
|-----|-------------|
| 50 pcs | $82.00/pc (with locating holes) |
| 200 pcs | $65.00/pc |
| 500 pcs | $60.00/pc |

This is a win-win: the locating holes improve consistency AND 
reduce cost for both sides. Sound good?
```

### 场景4: "What happens if quality is not acceptable?"

**预判概率**: ⭐⭐⭐ 中

```
Hi Jeremy,

Great question — here's our quality guarantee:

1. First Article: Before production, we send a first article 
   for your approval. Production only starts after you sign off.

2. In-process: We inspect critical dimensions at each setup change.

3. Final: 100% CMM inspection before shipment.

4. If any parts don't meet your drawing specifications:
   - We re-make them at our cost
   - Or issue a full refund
   - You keep any conforming parts

We've been ISO 9001 certified for [X] years and have served 
North American clients without a quality dispute. I can provide 
references from US customers if helpful.
```

### 场景5: "Can you hold inventory for us?"

**预判概率**: ⭐⭐⭐ 中（月产200-500件，可能需要安全库存）

```
Hi Jeremy,

Yes, we offer a consignment inventory program for regular customers:

Option A: Safety Stock
- We maintain 50-100 pcs of finished goods in our facility
- Ships within 3 days of your PO
- No extra cost — just a rolling order commitment

Option B: Blanket Order + Scheduled Releases
- You place a quarterly blanket PO (e.g., 600 pcs/quarter)
- Release shipments monthly or as needed
- Price locked for the quarter

This gives you the responsiveness of local inventory without 
the carrying cost. Interested?
```
