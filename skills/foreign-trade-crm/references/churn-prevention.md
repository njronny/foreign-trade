# 客户流失预警&挽回

## 流失预警信号

### 🔴 高危信号（立即行动）
- 超过60天未回复任何邮件/消息
- 取消已确认的订单
- 明确说"我们换了供应商"
- 欠款不付

### 🟡 中危信号（密切关注）
- 超过30天未回复
- 订单量突然减少50%+
- 回复变慢（从1天变成1周）
- 不再主动联系（之前会）

### 🟢 低危信号（保持关注）
- 沟通频率降低
- 不再问技术问题
- 邮件打开但未回复
- LinkedIn不再互动

## 挽回策略

### 策略1: 换渠道触达
邮件不回 → LinkedIn
LinkedIn不回 → WhatsApp/电话
都联系不上 → 官网表单留言

```
Hi [Name],

Tried reaching you via email but haven't heard back. 
Just wanted to make sure everything is OK on your end.

If you're no longer looking for [产品], no worries at all — 
just let me know so I stop bothering you! 😊

Best,
[Name]
```

### 策略2: 提供新价值
```
Hi [Name],

It's been a while! Thought you'd be interested in this:

[分享一个新的能力/认证/案例]

We've recently [新增能力/拿到认证/完成项目], which 
might be relevant to your work.

No response needed — just sharing.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 策略3: 找到流失原因
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed we haven't connected recently. As we're always 
looking to improve, I'd love to get your feedback:

Was there something we could have done better? 
- Pricing?
- Quality?
- Communication?
- Lead time?

Your honest feedback would be really valuable, even if 
we're not working together right now.

Thanks,
[Name]
```

### 策略4: 特殊优惠挽回
```
Hi [Name],

Good news — we've optimized our [工艺] process and can 
now offer [产品] at [X]% lower cost with the same quality.

New pricing:
| Qty | Old Price | New Price | Savings |
|-----|----------|----------|---------|
| 200 | $XX | $XX | -XX% |

This is a limited offer for valued customers. 
Interested in a new quote?

Best,
[Name]
```

### 策略5: 优雅留门
```
Hi [Name],

Completely understand if things have changed on your end. 
We'll keep your info on file and won't reach out again 
unless you contact us first.

That said, our door is always open. If you ever need a 
reliable backup supplier, you know where to find us. 

Wishing you and [Company] all the best!

Warm regards,
[Name]
```
（注意：这封"告别邮件"的回复率出奇的高）

## 挽回成功后的注意事项

1. **找到流失原因并解决** — 否则会再次流失
2. **不要提过去的矛盾** — 翻篇，向前看
3. **提供比之前更好的服务** — 证明回来的决定是对的
4. **密切跟进前3次订单** — 确保质量无可挑剔
