# 跟进节奏管理

## 全生命周期跟进时间线

```
Day 0: 发送开发信
Day 3: 跟进1（附加价值）
Day 7: 跟进2（案例）
Day 11: 跟进3（免费样品提议）
Day 14: 跟进4（告别邮件）
     ↓ 回复了
Day 15: 确认需求+追问缺失信息
Day 16-18: 技术评估+成本核算
Day 19: 发送报价单
Day 22: 跟进报价反馈
Day 25: 再次跟进（补充信息）
     ↓ 客户还价/谈判
Day 26-35: 多轮谈判
     ↓ 达成一致
Day 36: 确认订单（PI发出）
Day 37: 收到定金 → 安排生产
     ↓ 生产中
每周: 进度更新（照片+报告）
     ↓ 生产完成
Day XX: 发货前照片确认
Day XX+1: 发货+快递单号
Day XX+7: 确认收到
Day XX+14: 确认质量满意
Day XX+30: 询问是否有新需求
     ↓ 复购阶段
每月: 定期沟通
每季: 推荐新品/新能力
```

## 不同情况下的跟进话术

### 报价后无回复（Day 3）
```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on the quotation I sent on [date]. 
Let me know if you have any questions or need clarification 
on any line items.

I'm also happy to schedule a quick call to walk through 
the quote if that's helpful.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

### 报价后无回复（Day 7）
```
Hi [Name],

I understand you're busy. Just wanted to share a relevant 
resource — here's a case study of a similar [industry] project 
we completed recently:

[简短案例描述，3-4句]

Even if the timing isn't right for this order, I thought 
you might find it useful.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 样品确认后无回复（Day 7）
```
Hi [Name],

Hope you've had a chance to evaluate the samples. 

The CMM report showed all dimensions within specification, 
but I'd love to hear your assessment — especially on:
- Fit with mating components
- Surface finish satisfaction
- Any adjustments needed

If everything looks good, we can start production planning 
immediately. Current lead time for [数量] pcs is [X] days.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

### 交付后跟进（Day 14）
```
Hi [Name],

It's been about 2 weeks since you received the shipment. 
Everything working well?

Two quick questions:
1. How did the parts perform in your assembly?
2. Any feedback for improvement?

If you're planning your next order, our current lead time 
is [X] days. Happy to hold a production slot for you.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 复购提醒（根据消耗周期）
```
Hi [Name],

Based on your last order of [数量] pcs on [date], you might 
be running low on [产品] soon.

Just a heads up:
- Current pricing: $XX/pc (same as last order)
- Lead time: [X] days
- We have material in stock for quick turnaround

Want me to prepare a new quote?

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## 跟进注意事项

1. **每次跟进提供新价值** — 不是简单的"check in"
2. **不咄咄逼人** — 客户不回复可能有原因
3. **记录所有沟通** — 即使是"未回复"也记录
4. **根据客户节奏调整** — 有些客户喜欢每周联系，有些觉得烦
5. **多渠道但不骚扰** — 邮件→LinkedIn→电话，逐步升级
