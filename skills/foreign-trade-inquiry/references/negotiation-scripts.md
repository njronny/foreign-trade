# 多轮谈判话术库

## 场景1: "Your price is too high"

### ❌ 错误回复
"Ok, we can give you a discount."（直接降价=承认报价虚高）

### ✅ 正确回复
```
Hi [Name],

I understand price is important. Let me break down what's included in our quote:

1. Material: [材料] at market price — we don't compromise on material quality
2. Machining: [工艺] with [X]-axis CNC, holding ±[X]mm tolerance
3. Quality: 100% dimensional inspection + CMM report with every shipment
4. Service: English-speaking PM, 24-hour response time

If you've received a lower quote, I'd be happy to do an apples-to-apples comparison. Sometimes the difference comes down to:
- Material grade (are they using the exact spec?)
- Inspection level (do they include CMM reports?)
- Surface finish (are they achieving the Ra you specified?)

We can also explore cost-saving options:
- Adjust tolerance on non-critical features
- Increase quantity for volume discount
- Modify design for better machinability

Would you like to discuss any of these options?
```

## 场景2: "Can you match $X per piece?"

### ✅ 回复
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for sharing that target. To give you an honest assessment, I'd need to understand what's included in that price:

- Is that FOB or delivered?
- Does it include full dimensional inspection?
- What's the material grade?
- What's the lead time?

We don't typically engage in pure price matching because it often means cutting corners somewhere. But I'm confident we can find a solution that works for both sides.

For example, if we [提出调整方案], we could get closer to your target while maintaining the quality your application requires.

Would you like to explore this?
```

## 谈判黄金法则

1. **永远不要在没有理由的情况下降价** — 每次让步都要换取点什么
2. **先谈价值，再谈价格** — 客户理解了价值，价格就不是问题
3. **用问题代替拒绝** — "Why do you need X?" 比 "No" 更有效
4. **制造紧迫感** — "This pricing is valid for 30 days" / "Material prices are rising"
5. **让客户觉得自己赢了** — 给一个小的让步，让客户有成就感
6. **记录每次让步** — 确保"这次特殊"不会变成"每次都这样"
